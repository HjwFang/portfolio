Overview

WatsApp is a course community application designed to help University of Waterloo students connect, collaborate, and communicate within their academic courses. The platform addresses the fragmentation of student communication by providing a centralized hub for course-specific interactions, resource sharing, and study group coordination.

WatsApp provides a dedicated platform where students can join course-specific communities, share notes, organize study groups, and collaborate on projects in one centralized location.
